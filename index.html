Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech, advised by Dr. Chandan Reddy. The focus of my research is representation learning and hyperbolic networks, specifically in the contexts of graph processing and NLP. I also actively collaborate with Amazon Search to apply my solutions at scale in real-world E-commerce problems. My current aim is to explore the different usecases for non-Euclidean deep learning and make it a part of mainstream ML development.
Prior to my Ph.D., I received my Masters in Computational Linguistics and Bachelors in Computer Science from IIIT, Hyderabad.
